Atlas of Landscapes

Final Project for Ironhack Web Dev Bootcamp

Landscape emerges from the interaction of diverse geographical, cartographic and poetical agents which intervene in its construction, and which have a reflection in the tangible space.

Atlas of landscapes is an app that allows you to show these invisible agents in a concrete landscape, simply uploading a photo of this landscape.

The app extracts the geographical information from the metadata of the image, and then shows a series of dinamic maps that give us a multiple and complex vision of the territory that we inhabit.

Community Plans

Second Project of Ironhack Web Dev Bootcamp

Common Plans is a platform for citizen participation for the city of Madrid, in which it is possible to decide a use for vacant properties of the city council.

The app allows any citizen to propose a cultural event in one of these empty places. Collective intelligence is used this way to reactivate these spaces and to perform activities in benefit of the community.

Evacuation Challenge Game

First Project of Ironhack Web Dev Bootcamp

Evacuation Challenge is the first coding project I've faced. It is a game for two players written entirely in native javascript.

Each player must rescue a maximum number of people from a burning building. To do that, he has to break thin walls by clicking them and helps people reach the exit. A certain amount of clicks is restricted for each level and time is so short, so he should hurry up!

Interior Architecture for offices in Madrid

Geometry Studies for Polytechnic Museum of Moscow

Technical Consultancy for SBP

El Museo Politécnico de Moscú es un edificio histórico que ha sido lugar de atracción para amantes de la ciencia y la tecnología de todo el país.

A partir de 2011, se propone la actualización de su imagen y el cierre de sus dos grandes patios, norte y sur, por medio de dos cubiertas transparentes y de aspecto contemporáneo. Se realizan diversos estudios de geometría, optimización de recursos estructurales, imagen exterior y experiencia atmosférica interior.

Designing the Anthropocene Territory

Resilience Landscape in Upper Guadiana River Basin

Human behaviour on territory has had a great impact on the geophysical processes in last centuries, creating a new geological epoch: the Anthropocene. The environment exploitation in favour of productive sectors supposes an impoverishment of natural systems and debilitates biological relations. That generates a serious risk of desertification.

Therefore it becomes necessary to define a new paradigm which achieves a constructive engagement between technology and nature. A concept that reduces the pressure on the environment and increases our ability to anticipate.
